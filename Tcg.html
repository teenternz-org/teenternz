ABOUT -

The 3 of us started the Chattergals community to introduce a much needed dialogue amongst us girls. We felt that as friends what we discussed on a daily basis amongst us, were actually topics which most girls find difficult to address. So we created this community to address all problems teenage girls go through and to provide a platform to discuss topics that maybe taboo and also the fun stuff.
