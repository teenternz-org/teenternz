ABOUT -

Bizness is a youth led platform, from university and high school students. Our goal is to create a network of people who share the same passion and interests. Through our various programs which include: events, courses, projects a newsletter and magazine our audience and volunteers gain a real life experience coupled with knowledge in the fields of business, economics and entrepreneurship.
