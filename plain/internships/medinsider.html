Med Insider is a student-led medical newspaper that seeks to increase scientific literacy in the U.S. With reliable journalism, our team reports on important medical advances and is up to date with the medical events happening all around the world. We also seek to engage the community by connecting with people on the field and bringing awareness of the wonders of medicine!
