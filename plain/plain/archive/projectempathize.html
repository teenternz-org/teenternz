PROJECT EMPATHIZE

ABOUT -

We are a youth-led organisation based in 3 counties, namely Malayisa, India and Argentina. Project Empathize's aim is to create safe spaces through peer support communities for the youths to share, learn and grow together as a community as well as to create more empathetic people in our communities. We also are striving to destigmatize mental health among the youths from across the world
