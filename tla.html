THE LITERARY ACADEMIA

ABOUT -

The Literary Academia is a 501 c(3) youth-run non-profit created to promote and diversify the literary world. With an emphasis on uplifting writers and creating a close-knit group of avid readers, we plan to revolutionize the world of literature. All genres are welcome in this community!
