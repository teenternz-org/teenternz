MINDBOGGLE

ABOUT -

MindBoggle is an organization that's meant to inform students regarding STEM careers they may consider pursuing in the future. The goal is to allow young people striving for success in the STEM field to become familiar with the available opportunities and know which to go after. On top of that, there will also be posts containing cool science facts to show how learning can be entertaining!
