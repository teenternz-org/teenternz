ABOUT -

Touché, pronounced too-shay, is an independent organization which strives to provide news in an entertaining and fun way. Touché means to accept someone's remark as a Valid Point, hence the tagline 'Bas Valid Points'. Furthermore, As an active citizens community, we strive to make the youth more aware of their local environment, state, nation, and global world so that they can make informed decisions in the future.
